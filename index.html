Project Proposal

Project title: Gone Coding

Description: Gone Coding is a beginner-friendly coding website targeted to students and professionals with non-technical backgrounds who want to learn coding. Users will be asked about their career path/major, and depending on their choice, they will be given one or two language tutorials to choose from. The tutorials will provide a background about the coding language and how it relates to the career path/major, and include beginner level problems.

Target audience: Students or professionals with non-technical backgrounds who want to learn coding in relation to their interests and career path.

Competition: Other coding-specific learning platforms - Leetcode, Codecademy, freeCodeCamp.

What sets us apart: Focus on non technical learners; most coding apps and platforms heavily focus on people who are already in a technical career path, which can be intimidating.
